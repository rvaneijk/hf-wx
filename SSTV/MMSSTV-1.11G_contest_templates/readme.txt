
  How to setup my MMSSTV template


 Copy the .MTM file into your MMSSTV folder.

  Open the MMSSTV program & click the "S.templates 1" tab
 Right-click in one of the template areas at the bottom
 select "load from file" and select the .MTM file.

  Once you have the file loaded into your templates
 Double-click on it to load it.

  Now, to change your QRA,
 in the main image window, Double-click the text
 "Your report is 595 to IO91tk"

You can put your own QRA in the text box that appears.

Now, finally, to save your changes,
 deselect, by clicking in the middle of the main window
and drag the main window, back to one of the templates.
