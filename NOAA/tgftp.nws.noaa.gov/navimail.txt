NAVIMAIL


Météo-France's NAVIMAIL system enables you to receive gridded binary
(GRIB  files) for wind, pressure, waves, sea surface temperature, as
well as text  bulletins and satellite images.  There is a service
charge for GRIB data,  however, text bulletins and satellite images are
available at no charge. For  further information see:

http://www.meteo.fr/meteonet/services/navimail/navimail.html

The following information was obtained from the NAVIMAIL webpage and by
running  the Météo-France's 's NAVIMAIL program.  Errors are likely.
For questions or comments on NAVIMAIL, contact Météo-France,
navihelp@meteo.fr, using the  word "Help" in the subject line.

For GMDSS information, all the messages of the same METARERA are
generally  included in the same package. The complete list is available below: 

Example:

Send an e-mail to:   navimail@meteo.fr 
Subject line:        Put anything you like
Body:                @mto@reqt@bull@
                     GMDSS_METAREA4_INMARSAT
                     SAT_AMERIQUE_NORD
                     @mto@reqt@fin@



****COMMANDS****

@mto@reqt@bull@     (Required Header) 
@mto@reqt@fin@      (Required Trailer) 
noattach            (Place requested bulletin in body of e-mail 
                     rather than as attached file)
